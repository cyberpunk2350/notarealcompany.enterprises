Welcome to Not A Real Company (NARC)

We build polished, enterprise-style homelab environments and documentation — with a wink. From internal DNS to faux SaaS dashboards, we make fake work feel impressively real.

Who we are

NARC is a homelab-based faux enterprise created to practice infrastructure, documentation, and corporate processes. We build training scenarios, prototypes, and the occasional overly ornate flowchart.

Official NARC Memo: Please ensure your imaginary badge is visible at all times. The Compliance Bot is watching gently.

Mission

To create a realistic corporate sandbox where learning, experimentation, and well-documented chaos can thrive.

Vision

To be the most playfully bureaucratic homelab—where every simulated ticket is treated like a mission-critical incident.

Values
